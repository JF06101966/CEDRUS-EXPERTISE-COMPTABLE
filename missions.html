Our Services

Comprehensive accounting expertise for your business

Accounting

Accounting is far more than a regulatory obligation: it is an essential management tool. At CEDRUS, we provide rigorous and personalised management of your accounting and tax obligations.

Tax filing & annual accounts

Balance sheet, income statement, and notes forming a complete tax filing package, excluding dissolution or liquidation operations.

Tax returns

Handling of VAT returns, corporate tax filings, and other mandatory declarations, according to your business activity and tax regime.

Bookkeeping

Entry and processing of purchase invoices, sales invoices, and expenses, using paperless tools.

Bank reconciliation

Regular verification of consistency between bank statements and accounting records.

Tax, legal, and social advisory

Tax optimisation, situation analysis, and legal levers: tax credits, arbitrage, and regime selection.

Payroll & HR

Pre-employment declarations (DPAE)

Payslips

Social declarations (URSSAF, pension funds, insurance, mutual fund)

Drafting of employment contracts

Maintenance of individual employee records
