About Us

An experienced and innovative team dedicated to your entrepreneurial success

Over 30 years of experience building a relationship of trust

Drawing on more than three decades of experience as a chartered accountant at the helm of a firm he founded, built, and later sold — advising over 800 clients alongside a team of nearly 45 collaborators — Jean-François Le Gall has chosen to open a new chapter by creating CEDRUS Expertise Comptable & Conseils, a 100% digital firm entirely dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in accounting expertise.

Designed as a next-generation firm, CEDRUS stands out through its ability to meet the evolving expectations of entrepreneurs, executives and business founders, in an economic environment that is increasingly complex and uncertain, yet particularly rich in opportunities.

These three decades of advisory work have also forged a deep attachment to the Middle East and its people, with whom a genuine relationship of trust has been built over time. CEDRUS intends to extend and deepen this privileged connection, by supporting these entrepreneurs and investors in the development and success of their projects in France.

Our approach: Rooted & Agile

We are rooted in the fundamentals of the profession: rigour, compliance, adherence to deadlines, and regulatory expertise. But we are also agile, capable of rethinking our methods, embracing technological innovation, and delivering organisational models that are more streamlined, collaborative, and high-performing.

Rooted

  • Rigour and compliance across all our work
  • Strict adherence to deadlines and schedules
  • Thorough command of regulatory requirements
  • Recognised technical expertise

Agile

  • Continuous technological innovation
  • Adaptive working methods
  • Collaborative and streamlined organisation
  • Maximum responsiveness and flexibility
