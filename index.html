Our Vision

Founded by Jean-Francois Le Gall with over 30 years of expertise, CEDRUS was built as a gateway for international investors and entrepreneurs looking to establish or grow their business in France. We understand the challenges of navigating French tax and social security law, as well as the critical choice of the right corporate structure — a dense field where every decision impacts taxation, the founder's social protection and the long-term viability of the project. Our firm bridges the gap between your ambitions and the French business landscape, providing end-to-end accounting, tax advisory, social compliance and strategic support so you can invest and build with confidence.

How it works

A straightforward process in 4 steps, from your first visit to day-to-day management

Describe your situation

Step 2
